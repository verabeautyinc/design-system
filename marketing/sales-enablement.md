## 4. Battlecard — Competitor Comparison

### Vera vs. Key Competitors

| Feature | Vera | RealSelf | Zocdoc | Groupon/Deals | Instagram |
|---------|------|----------|--------|---------------|-----------|
| **Provider verification** | Independent board cert + license verification | Self-reported credentials | Basic insurance verification | None | None |
| **Treatment planning** | Personalized plans based on skin profile, MST | None — directory/review only | None — booking only | None — deal only | None |
| **Clinical evidence** | Peer-reviewed citations on every treatment | User-generated content | None | None | Influencer opinions |
| **Pricing transparency** | Real patient transaction data | Provider-submitted ranges | Insurance-based | Deal price (not standard) | Not available |
| **Review verification** | Verified patient visits only | Mostly unverified | Verified visits | Purchase-verified | Unverified |
| **Skin tone personalization** | 10-point Monk Skin Tone scale | None | None | None | None |
| **Paid placements** | None — merit-based only | Yes — sponsored listings | Yes — promoted results | Yes — pay-to-play | Yes — paid promotion |
| **Patient quality** | Pre-researched, treatment plan in hand | Varies widely | Varies | Deal-seekers (low retention) | Varies — often impulse |
| **Cost to providers** | Booking fee (no listing fee) | Subscription + per-lead fees | Per-booking fee | Deep discount required | Ad spend |
| **Show rate** | 92% | ~80% | ~78% | ~65% | N/A |

### Key Differentiators to Emphasize

1. **"No paid placements"** — this is our strongest trust signal for providers who are tired of pay-to-play platforms
2. **"Pre-researched patients"** — Vera patients have already reviewed clinical evidence and pricing; they arrive informed
3. **"Skin tone personalization"** — no competitor does this; it's a unique value for providers serving diverse populations
4. **"92% show rate"** — informed patients cancel less; this directly impacts provider revenue

### Objection Responses (Quick Reference)

| They say... | You say... |
|-------------|-----------|
| "We already get patients from RealSelf" | "RealSelf sends you patients who've read reviews. Vera sends you patients who've built a treatment plan with clinical evidence. They arrive more informed, convert higher, and cancel less." |

| "What about Groupon patients?" | "Deal-seekers have the lowest retention rate in the industry (~15%). Vera patients come for a plan, not a discount. Our retention rate is 4x higher." |

## 5. Product Demo Script

**Duration:** 12 minutes
**Audience:** Practice owner/manager, 1-on-1 or small group

### Opening (2 min)

> "Thanks for taking the time. I want to show you something different from the usual marketing platforms you've seen.
>
> Quick question before I start — where do most of your new patients come from today? [Listen]
>
> [Acknowledge their answer.] That's common. What I hear from most providers is that the patients who find them through [platform they mentioned] are often poorly informed, price-shopping, or cancel at high rates.
>
> Vera is designed to solve that. Let me show you how."

### Patient Experience (4 min)

> "Let me show you what your future patient sees."
>
> [Walk through: Skin profile creation → Treatment plan generation → Provider matching → Provider profile view → Booking flow]
>
> "Notice a few things:
> - She built a treatment plan before she ever saw your name. She's not browsing — she's deciding.
> - Your profile shows your verified credentials, real patient reviews, and transparent pricing.
> - She knows exactly what to expect before she books.
>
> This is why Vera patients show up prepared."

### Provider Dashboard (4 min)

> [Walk through: Provider dashboard → Booking management → Patient skin profile view → Review management → Analytics]
>
> "When a patient books through Vera, you see their complete skin profile — tone, concerns, goals, and treatment plan. You know what they're expecting before they walk in.
>
> Your reviews are from verified patient visits only. No anonymous attacks, no purchased reviews.
>
> And your ranking is based on patient outcomes and satisfaction — not how much you spend on advertising."

### Close (2 min)

> "Here's the bottom line:
>
> Vera sends you patients who've already done their research, understand their treatment plan, and chose you specifically for your verified expertise.
>
> There's no listing fee. You pay a booking fee only when a patient actually books.
>
> Verification takes 48 hours. I can start that process today if you'd like.
>
> What questions do you have?"

## 6. Objection Handling Guide (10 Common Objections)

| # | Objection | Response |
|---|-----------|----------|
| 1 | **"We don't need more patients."** | "This isn't about more patients — it's about better patients. Vera patients arrive informed, with a treatment plan, and a 92% show rate. The question isn't volume; it's quality." |
| 2 | **"What's the cost?"** | "Zero to join. Zero listing fee. You pay a booking fee only when a patient books and shows up. If they cancel, you don't pay. We only make money when you do." |
| 3 | **"How is this different from Zocdoc?"** | "Zocdoc books appointments. Vera builds treatment plans. Patients don't just find you — they arrive having reviewed clinical evidence, understood pricing, and built a personalized plan. The conversion and retention rates reflect that difference." |
| 4 | **"I'm worried about negative reviews."** | "Every review on Vera comes from a verified patient visit. No anonymous attacks, no competitor sabotage. And because our patients arrive well-informed, they have realistic expectations — which leads to higher satisfaction scores." |
| 5 | **"Transparent pricing will undercut my rates."** | "Actually, the opposite. Patients who see transparent pricing self-select based on value, not just cost. Providers with higher pricing but strong reviews and credentials often outperform cheaper alternatives on Vera. Transparency rewards quality." |
| 6 | **"We already have a strong online presence."** | "That's great — and Vera complements it. Think of us as an additional channel that specifically attracts high-intent, pre-researched patients. Most providers see Vera patients as incremental to their existing channels, not a replacement." |
| 7 | **"How do you verify providers?"** | "We independently confirm board certification and active license status with state medical boards and certifying organizations. It's not self-reported. This protects both patients and legitimate providers like you." |
| 8 | **"What if I don't like the patients I get?"** | "Because patients build a treatment plan before booking, there's a natural fit. They're not browsing — they're looking for a provider who matches their specific needs. You can also set preferences for the types of treatments you want to be matched for." |
| 9 | **"This sounds like a lot of work to set up."** | "Setup takes about 20 minutes. We handle the credential verification (48 hours). You set your pricing, availability, and treatment specialties. After that, it runs itself — bookings come to you through the dashboard or your existing calendar integration." |
| 10 | **"I'll think about it."** | "Absolutely. Here's what I'd suggest: I'll send you our provider one-pager and a link to see how your profile would look. No commitment. Verification is free and takes 48 hours — you can always pause or deactivate your profile if it's not working for you." |
