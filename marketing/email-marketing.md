**Subject:** What Korean dermatologists know that you don't
**Preview:** What 89% of women researching treatments wish they knew.

**Body copy:**

> **In Korea, skincare isn't about fixing problems. It's about prevention.**
>
> While the Western beauty industry sells you "anti-aging" — implying aging is something to fight — Korean dermatology takes a different approach. They call it _피부관리_ (pibu-gwanli): skin management.
>
> The difference?
>
> | Anti-aging approach    | Skin management approach |
> | ---------------------- | ------------------------ |
> | React to problems      | Prevent problems         |
> | One dramatic treatment | A planned series         |
> | Based on trends        | Based on evidence        |
> | Same for everyone      | Tailored to your skin    |
>
> This is the philosophy behind Vera. We don't believe in "fixes." We believe in plans — thoughtful, personalized, evidence-based treatment plans that work with your skin's biology, not against it.
>
> **Want to learn more?**
>
> [Read: The Science of Preventive Skincare →]

**CTA:** Read article → Blog/content page

### Nurture 2: "Choosing a Provider" (Day 12)

**Subject:** Before you book that Botox appointment
**Preview:** No hidden prices. No fake reviews. No pressure.

**Body copy:**

> **5 questions to ask before you book any aesthetic treatment:**
>
> 1. **Is the provider board-certified?** Not just "certified" — board-certified in dermatology or plastic surgery. There's a difference.
>
> 2. **Can I see real patient reviews?** Not testimonials on their website. Independent reviews from verified patients.
>
> 3. **What does it actually cost?** Including follow-ups, touch-ups, and aftercare. The first session is never the full picture.
>
> 4. **What does the clinical evidence say?** Not "it works great!" but actual study data on efficacy rates and side effects.
>
> 5. **Is this right for my skin tone?** Some treatments perform differently across skin tones. Make sure your provider has experience with yours.
>
> On Vera, every provider profile answers all five questions before you even have to ask.
>
> [Find Verified Providers →]

**CTA:** Find Verified Providers → Provider search

## Email Design Specifications

| Element      | Specification                                                            |
| ------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Max width    | 600px                                                                    |
| Background   | White (`#FFFFFF`)                                                        |
| Body font    | Geist Regular 400, 16px, `#44403C` (Neutral-700)                         |
| Heading font | Geist SemiBold 600, 20px, `#1C1917` (Neutral-900)                        |
| Link color   | `#D85959` (Primary-500)                                                  |
| CTA button   | `#D85959` bg, white text, full radius, 48px height, full width           |
| Dividers     | 1px `#E7E5E4` (Neutral-200)                                              |
| Header       | Vera wordmark, centered, 100px wide                                      |
| Footer       | `#A8A29E` (Neutral-400), 12px, centered                                  |
| Spacing      | 24px between sections, 16px between paragraphs                           |
| Images       | Avoid — text-only emails load faster and render consistently             |
| Dark mode    | Email clients auto-invert; test in Apple Mail, Gmail, Outlook dark modes |
