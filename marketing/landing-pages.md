### Testimonial Framework

Each testimonial must include:

| Element | Format | Example |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Quote | 2-3 sentences, first person | "I spent months researching online and felt more confused every day. Vera gave me a clear plan in 5 minutes." |
| Name | First name + last initial | J.K. |
| Age | Actual age | 41 |
| Treatment | What they explored/booked | Botox + Microneedling |
| Skin tone | MST reference (optional, if consented) | MST 06 |

**Diversity requirement:** Minimum 3 testimonials visible, representing at least 3 different MST tones, 3 different age brackets (35–39, 40–45, 46–55), and 3 different treatment types.

**Do not:**
- Use stock testimonials or fabricated quotes
- Include photos without explicit consent
- Show before/after imagery in testimonials
- Use superlatives ("the best," "amazing," "life-changing")

## 5. FAQ Section

### Section heading: Questions? Answers.

| # | Question | Answer |
|---|----------|--------|
| 1 | **What is Vera?** | Vera is a clinical beauty platform that helps you make informed decisions about aesthetic treatments. We provide personalized treatment plans, verified provider listings, and clinical evidence — all in one place. |
| 2 | **Is Vera free?** | Yes. Creating a skin profile and building your treatment plan is completely free. You only pay when you book a treatment with a provider — and you'll see the exact cost before you book. |
| 3 | **How are providers verified?** | Every provider on Vera has their board certification and license status independently confirmed. We also verify that reviews come from patients who actually visited the provider. We don't accept paid placements. |
| 4 | **What makes Vera different from RealSelf or Yelp?** | Unlike review sites, Vera doesn't just list providers — we build you a personalized treatment plan based on your skin profile and goals. Every treatment recommendation is backed by peer-reviewed clinical studies, and every provider is independently verified. |
| 5 | **Does Vera work for all skin tones?** | Yes. Vera uses the 10-point Monk Skin Tone scale to personalize treatment recommendations. Some treatments perform differently across skin tones, and we account for that in our evidence-based recommendations. |
| 6 | **Do I have to book through Vera?** | No. You can use Vera purely for research and planning. When you're ready to book, we connect you directly with verified providers — but there's no pressure and no timeline. |
| 7 | **What treatments does Vera cover?** | Vera covers non-surgical aesthetic treatments including injectables (Botox, fillers), skin resurfacing (chemical peels, microneedling, laser), and preventive skincare treatments. We're expanding our coverage regularly. |
| 8 | **Is my data private?** | Absolutely. Your skin profile, treatment plan, and personal information are never shared with providers or third parties without your explicit consent. We don't sell your data. Period. |

**Visual direction:**
- Accordion style (Accordion component from design system)
- Question: Callout / SemiBold, Neutral-900
- Answer: Body / Regular, Neutral-600
- 1px Neutral-200 dividers between items
- Expand/collapse with chevron icon
