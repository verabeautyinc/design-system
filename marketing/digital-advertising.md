# Digital Advertising — Awareness Campaign

**Campaign:** "Age Beautifully"
**Objective:** Brand awareness among women 35–55 researching aesthetic treatments
**Tone:** Calm confidence. No urgency. No discounts. No fear.

---

#### Story Ad 2 — "Skin Close-Up"

**Sequence (video, 6 seconds):**

- Opens on an extreme close-up of real skin (pores visible, natural texture, warm light)
- Slowly pulls back to reveal a woman's face, calm, looking directly at camera
- Text overlays in sequence: "Your skin tells a story." → "Make sure it's a good one."

**Visual direction:** Shot on macro lens. Warm, natural light. The honesty of showing real skin texture is the differentiator. Music: soft, ambient, Korean-inspired instrumental.

**A/B test:** Close-up opening vs. direct-to-face shot. Test whether the macro skin texture hook drives higher view-through rates.

#### Story Ad 3 — "The Scroll"

**Sequence (video, 8 seconds):**

- Screen recording simulation: someone scrolling through confusing beauty content — conflicting advice, fake reviews, hidden prices
- Screen "glitches" and clears to a clean Vera interface
- Text: "Finally. A platform you can trust."
- Vera wordmark + CTA

**Visual direction:** Chaotic scroll portion uses desaturated, slightly harsh lighting. Vera interface portion is clean, warm, calm. The contrast is the story.

**A/B test:** "Finally. A platform you can trust." vs "Cut through the noise." — test emotional vs functional framing.

## Campaign Targeting

### Audience Segments

| Segment            | Age   | Interests                                        | Platform Priority         |
| ------------------ | ----- | ------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------- |
| "Skin-curious"     | 35–45 | Skincare, dermatology, K-beauty, wellness        | Instagram, TikTok         |
| "Treatment-ready"  | 40–55 | Botox, fillers, aesthetic treatments, med spas   | Google Search, Facebook   |
| "Wellness-forward" | 35–50 | Health, fitness, preventive care, organic beauty | Instagram, Google Display |

### Exclusions

- Exclude audiences interested in cosmetic surgery (too invasive for our positioning)
- Exclude audiences under 25 (wrong life stage)
- Exclude males for initial awareness campaign (secondary audience, later phase)

### Budget Allocation (Awareness Phase)

| Channel                            | Allocation | Rationale                                           |
| ---------------------------------- | ---------- | --------------------------------------------------- |
| Instagram (Feed + Stories + Reels) | 40%        | Primary discovery channel for our audience          |
| Google Search                      | 25%        | Captures active intent ("best botox near me")       |
| TikTok                             | 20%        | Growing 35+ demographic, high organic amplification |
| Google Display                     | 10%        | Retargeting and brand reinforcement                 |
| Facebook Feed                      | 5%         | Supplementary reach for 45+ segment                 |
