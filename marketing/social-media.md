### Thread 2 — "5 Things to Know Before Your First Treatment"

**Tweet 1 (hook):**
> Thinking about your first aesthetic treatment?
>
> Here are 5 things your provider's website won't tell you.
>
> (Saving this thread = saving yourself hours of research.) 🧵

**Tweet 2:**
> 1. "Board-certified" ≠ "certified."
>
> A "certified injector" may have taken a weekend course. A board-certified dermatologist or plastic surgeon has 12+ years of training.
>
> Always ask: board-certified in WHAT?

**Tweet 3:**
> 2. Your skin tone affects your results.
>
> Darker skin tones (MST 06-10) have higher risks of hyperpigmentation with certain laser treatments. Lighter tones may need fewer sessions for some procedures.
>
> A good provider adjusts protocols by skin tone. A great one explains why.

**Tweet 4:**
> 3. The first treatment is rarely the last.
>
> Botox lasts 3-4 months. Filler lasts 6-18 months. Microneedling requires 3-6 sessions.
>
> Ask about the full treatment timeline and total cost — not just the first visit.

**Tweet 5:**
> 4. "Before and after" photos can lie.
>
> Different lighting, angles, makeup, and even time of day can dramatically change how skin looks in photos.
>
> Ask for photos taken under consistent clinical lighting. Or better yet, ask to see clinical study data.

**Tweet 6:**
> 5. You don't need to decide today.
>
> Any provider who pressures you to book on the spot is a red flag. Good medicine doesn't use sales tactics.
>
> Take your time. Do your research. The right treatment will still be there next week.

**Tweet 7 (close):**

## Content Pillars

All social content should map to one of these four pillars:

| Pillar | % of Content | Description | Example |
|--------|-------------|-------------|---------|
| **Educate** | 40% | Treatment science, skin facts, clinical evidence | Retinol vs. Tretinoin explainer |
| **Empower** | 25% | Decision-making tools, know-your-rights, red flags | Med spa red flags list |
| **Inspire** | 20% | Reframing aging, confidence, self-acceptance | "I'm 43 and I don't want to look 25" |
| **Trust** | 15% | Provider verification, transparency, platform values | "We don't sell treatments" |

Never post content that falls outside these pillars. If it doesn't educate, empower, inspire, or build trust — it doesn't belong on our channels.
